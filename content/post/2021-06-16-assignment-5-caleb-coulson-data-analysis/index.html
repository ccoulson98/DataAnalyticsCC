urbanization is characterized b the influx of people moving from rural areas to urban areas such as cities and towns. This was seen in the ameriacas and western europe after the industrial revolution. Many countries are just reaching this point of mass movement towards cities, and some have no reached it atall. This can have large effects on the GSP, and other aspects of a country, we will explore soe of these now. To explore this, i will use the urban population % as the indicator of urbanization First I will compare the GDP of the countries by their urban population %

A general trend upwards can be seen, however converting the y axis (GDP) to a log10 scale will make this more prominent, in addition to this I will add a trend line of the data to show the general trend of the data
